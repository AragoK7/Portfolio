General

My name is Goran Čogić
I was born on October, 1999 in Novi Sad, Serbia
Since I was born I spent a lot of time in front of computer, perhaps too much.
As a child and teenager, most of my time on computer was spent on video games.
In middle school I got introduced to programming where we studied C and C#.
Thanks to my older brother who finished the same school I went to, I already had a bit of programming knowledge, just enough to have easy time coding in school, but I wasn't crazy passionate about it.
The moment I got passion for programming was when I decided to quit faculty so I could focus most of my attention on it.
Beside spending a lot of time on computer, I also love doing sports, or any kind of physical activity. Since my early days, I was kinda competitive, so besides having fun, any activity that I do, I like to be at least good at, anything more is great, anything less, not so great :D
